Breakfast Soup

Home

Description

A controversial dish that pushes the boundaries of what food is. This recipe aims to both bring people together, and then divide them by asking the hard hitting questions: "what is soup?"

Ingredients

  • Bowl and spoon
  • Milk
  • Cereal of your choice
  • Friends

Steps

  1. Take out your bowl, spoon, milk and cereal
  2. Put your spoon in the bowl.
  3. Ask your friends if they put the milk in first or the cereal in first, discuss, add the groups consensus on what goes in first. Then add the other.
  4. Ask your friends if they think this is a soup, or some other type of food involving things suspended in a liquid broth.
  5. Optional topics of discussion include, are poptarts ravioli, when stacking 2 pieces of lasagna together, how many lasagna result, or any other food catergorical questions of your choice
