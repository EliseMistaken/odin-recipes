Bread Sandwich

Description

A british classic, known around the world for the austerity of the dish, the bread sandwich is an incredibly simple dish for those nights you're just feeling tuckered out.

Ingredients

  • 1x Slice of bread
  • 1x Slice of bread
  • 1x Slice of bread

Steps

  1. Place your third slice of bread on a plate
  2. Put the first slice of bread on top of the third slice. It's been on the outside of the loaf, let it be at the middle of the cuddle puddle
  3. Finally, Add the second slice on top to bring it all together.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
