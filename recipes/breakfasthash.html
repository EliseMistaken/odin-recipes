Breakfast Hash

Home

Description

A hash is a classic breakfast meal. Combining egg, potato, peppers and onion with some spicy chorizo provides a healthy, hearty start to your day. With simple methods and delicious results, try this one out!

Ingredients

  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 Yukon Gold potato
  • Bell pepper of choice
  • 8oz Mexican Chorizo
  • 1 TBSP butter or oil
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Salt, pepper and seasonings of your choice
  • (optional)Salsa or hot sauce
  • (optional)Avocado

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil, during that time dice your potatoes, onion and pepper
  2. Add the diced potatoes to the water to cook and soften. Once a fork can enter the outer portion of a potato, drain and set aside.
  3. Add the chorizo to a skillet, cook through over medium heat.
  4. Remove the chorizo and add diced peppers and onions. Add salt and pepper.Cook until soften and the onions are transparent
  5. Remove peppers and onions, add potatoes to the skillet. Add oil, salt and pepper to the pan and let on side crisp up.
  6. Add back chorizo, peppers and onions, allow to heat up, and add any additional seasoning
  7. Remove from pan, turn off heat and add eggs. Cook for 1 minute then flip, cook for an additional 30 seconds. Or however you like your eggs, I'm not the boss of you
